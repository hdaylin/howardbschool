VISIT HOWARD

Visiting Howard University provides students a chance to immerse you in the Howard Experience and to interact with our students, faculty and staff. Our campus visit program is designed to provide our guests with information about our wide range of academic programs, the admission process, financial aid, and student life on campus. Visitors will attend an information session lead by an Admission Recruiter or a Student Ambassador, followed by a walking tour of the Universityâ€™s main campus. The information session and campus tour last approximately two hours.

Campus visits are available Monday through Friday at select times throughout the day. Families and individuals interested in scheduling a campus visit can do so at 10 a.m., 12 p.m., and 2 p.m.

To reserve your date, please select:

  • Reserving Your Family/Individual Tour
  • Request Your Group Tour (for groups of 10 or more individuals)

NOTE:Campus tours and visits are suspended on the weekends, federal holidays, and for university closings. In addition, tours for the fall semester are scheduled beginning the week after Labor Day (September through November). In December, tours will be unavailable through mid-January. In the spring, tours are scheduled through April.
